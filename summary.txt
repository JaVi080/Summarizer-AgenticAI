- Artwork is a creative work made by people.  
- It expresses ideas, feelings, or tells stories.  
- Types include drawings, paintings, sculptures, photographs, and digital creations.  
- Created to share beauty, emotions, or messages.  
- Serves as a means of communication and expression.Animes:
- Anime is a style of animated shows and movies from Japan.  
- It features colorful artwork, exciting stories, and unique characters.  
- Anime covers many genres such as adventures, fantasy, friendships, and everyday life.  
- It is enjoyed by people of all ages and can be funny, dramatic, or scary.  
- Anime is similar to cartoons but has its own distinct style and storytelling.
