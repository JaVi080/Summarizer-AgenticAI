
Artificial Intelligence:

- Artificial intelligence (AI) enables computational systems to perform tasks typically associated with human intelligence.  
- These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and decision-making.  
- AI is a multidisciplinary field involving engineering, mathematics, and computer science.  
- It focuses on developing methods and software that allow machines to perceive their environment.  
- AI systems use learning and intelligence to take actions that maximize their chances of achieving specific goals.

Programming:

- **Program/Programme:** Refers to a planned series of events, shows, or activities.
- **Programmer:** A person who writes computer software.
- **Programming:** The process of writing and testing code that a computer executes.
- **Multiple Meanings:** The terms can relate to technology, television, events, or other organized sequences.
- **Context Dependent:** The exact meaning varies based on usage in different fields like computing, broadcasting, or event management.

Artwork:

- A work of art refers to an artistic creation with aesthetic value.
- "Work of art" can refer broadly to any artistic work, including literature and music, while other terms mainly denote tangible visual art forms.
- It includes fine art examples like paintings and sculptures.
- Decorative and applied arts objects, designed for both aesthetic appeal and functional use (e.g., jewelry, ceramics, folk art), are also considered artworks.
- Art can also encompass functional, religious, or conceptual objects that have gained artistic appreciation, as well as photographs, films, installation art, and conceptual art.

Animes:

- Anime is a type of cartoon originating from Japan.  
- It features colorful drawings and characters with big eyes and expressive faces.  
- Anime covers a wide range of themes such as adventures, friendships, magic, and everyday life.  
- It appeals to people of all ages due to its fun, exciting, and emotional stories.  
- Anime is a unique form of animated show or movie reflecting Japanese culture.
