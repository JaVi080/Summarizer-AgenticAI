
Artificial Intelligence:

- Artificial intelligence (AI) enables computational systems to perform tasks typically associated with human intelligence.  
- These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and decision-making.  
- AI is a multidisciplinary field involving engineering, mathematics, and computer science.  
- It focuses on developing methods and software that allow machines to perceive their environment.  
- AI systems use learning and intelligence to take actions that maximize their chances of achieving specific goals.

Programming:

- **Program/Programme:** Refers to a planned series of events, shows, or activities.
- **Programmer:** A person who writes computer software.
- **Programming:** The process of writing and testing code that a computer executes.
- **Multiple Meanings:** The terms can relate to technology, television, events, or other organized sequences.
- **Context Dependent:** The exact meaning varies based on usage in different fields like computing, broadcasting, or event management.
